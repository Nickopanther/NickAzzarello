About Me

Welcome to my very first website! On this website you get an inside look on my life regarding my jobs, interests, and myself. I was born on September 15, 1992 and grew up in Wernersville, PA. I have become fascinated with computers and video from a very young age. I've used the computer since I was 3 and started making videos (starting with VHS cassettes) at the age of 6. I attended school at Conrad Weiser from kindergarden through eighth grade and moved to the Governor Mifflin area during my high school years.


I graduated from high school, at Governor Mifflin, in 2011 and got a job as a cashier at Redner's and started my college education at RACC. After two years of RACC, I transfered to Kutztown University to major in Electronic Media. During my time up at Kutztown, I have worked on a lot of video projects from a bunch of classes. I got to write and produce my own short projects and even shot the college's basketball games. Before graduation I got an internship at TC Motion in Lancaster where I was a production assistant on multiple projects.


Now I am a recent graduate from Kutztown University with a bachelor's degree in Electronic Media. I am returning to RACC to get a certificate in Web Development as I work at my two new jobs, theReading Royalsand Fightin' Phils, as a camera operator. I have been teaching myself how to code from all sorts of languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have been practicing using Codecademy and reading several For Dummies books on coding. I am currently working on my certificate in Web Development at Reading Area Community College. For my major at Kutztown University, I have been involved in multiple productions from class assignments to outside work. I worked as a boom operator for a short film that would soon be entered in a contest. I also volunteered as a camera operator for BCTV and participated in a club that would broadcast the university's local news.


Projects from Kutztown University

At Kutztown University I have made numerous video projects with the knowledge based off what I learned from my classes. I had the chance to script, shoot, and edit many projects and helped out on other projects.


TC Motion Internship

In the summer of 2015, I applied for an internship at TC Motion in Lancaster, PA. During my internship, I observed and helped out on many commercial projects in the studio. I was a production assistant and key grip on all of their projects from lighting the set to changing the lens. It was an interesting and educational experience for me.
